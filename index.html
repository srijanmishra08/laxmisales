Material Availability

Single Crystals

Research quantities, prototype devices

High-purity single crystals grown using Bridgman-Stockbarger technique. Ideal for fundamental research, spectroscopy studies, and prototype device fabrication. Available in various orientations and sizes.

Polycrystalline Ingots

Substrate preparation, bulk studies

Dense polycrystalline ingots suitable for substrate preparation, thermal property measurements, and bulk material characterization. Cost-effective option for preliminary research.

Thin Films

Device integration, custom specs

Custom-deposited thin films on various substrates including glass, silicon, and flexible materials. Controlled thickness from nanometers to micrometers with excellent uniformity.

Powders

Precursor material, research applications

Finely ground semiconductor powders in various mesh sizes. Perfect as precursor materials for film deposition, chemical synthesis, or composite material development.

Nanostructures

Sensing, catalytic applications

Engineered nanoparticles and nanowires with controlled morphology. Enhanced surface area for catalysis, sensing applications, and quantum confinement studies.

Exfoliated Flakes

2D material research

Mechanically or chemically exfoliated 2D flakes for cutting-edge research in van der Waals heterostructures, optoelectronics, and fundamental 2D physics studies.
