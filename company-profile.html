Laxmi Sales founded in 2017, in Raipur, Chhattisgarh, India, is a reliable manufacturer, supplier, and trader.

Planning

We are very keen on strategic planning at every stage of our operation. From the procurement of raw materials to the production and delivery of finished products, we ensure that every process is meticulously planned for it to meet all of our customers' specifications and timelines.

We analyze market trends and the demanded requirements from customers and predict the required inventory and optimize production schedules. This forward-looking approach will help us remain ahead in the industry, giving timely and reliable products to our clients.

Inventory Management

Inventory management is a very important aspect of our business model. We have a system of organizing and managing our warehouse such that we are able to track stock levels, monitor the quality of our products, and undertake quick turnarounds.

In general, our inventory management system helps us make predictions about orders by avoiding stockouts and streamlining orders so that customer requirements are fulfilled as efficiently as possible. Be it bulk or specialized request, our inventory system allows us to do this on time and get back to customers quickly.
