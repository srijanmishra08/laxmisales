About Grey Alumina, Refractory Alumina

Grey Alumina Powder, also known as grey aluminum oxide (Al2O3), is a versatile material used in various industries, including ceramics, electronics, abrasives and catalysts.

  • Alumina is a key component in manufacturing high-temperature and wear-resistant ceramic products like industrial ceramics, refractory bricks and furnace linings.
  • Alumina's high hardness and abrasive properties make it suitable for polishing and grinding applications, including metal polishing and as a component in sandpaper.
  • Alumina is used in coatings to protect against abrasion.
  • Alumina is used in the production of lithium-ion batteries.
  • Alumina is used to remove water from gas streams.
